    About VESPER

    VESPER is a one-touch arcade survival game built to run entirely in the browser — no install, no account, no server. Every shard, every high score and every unlocked skin lives in your device's local storage.

    The pitch was simple: take the instant readability of one-touch classics, the tight hitboxes of a hardcore dodger, and the escalating tension of a survival loop, then wrap it in a shaft you fall through forever. There's no ending to reach — only a further personal best.

    Built with plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No game engine, no build step.

    Privacy

    VESPER collects nothing. There is no server, no analytics, and no account system.

    Your scores, coins, XP, achievements and settings are stored only in your browser's local storage, on your device. Clearing your browser data or using "Reset Progress" removes it permanently. Nothing is ever transmitted anywhere.
